About Me

My journey in technology began with a fascination for how things work behind the screen. Armed with an M.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology—and a University Gold Medal in hand—I dove into the world of web development, where I discovered my passion for creating dynamic, user-focused applications. Over the last three years, I’ve honed my skills as a Full Stack Developer, specializing in PHP, Laravel, and JavaScript. From developing custom CRM solutions to optimizing complex internal applications, I've worked with a range of tools and frameworks that bring efficiency to each project I tackle.

Starting as a backend developer, I later expanded my expertise to front-end frameworks, like ReactJS, and content management systems, including WordPress. Each role has allowed me to solve unique problems and deliver meaningful results for my clients.

With a strong foundation in web development, my technical expertise includes:

  • Languages & Frameworks: PHP, JavaScript, ReactJS, Laravel, Vue (basic knowledge)
  • Content Management: WordPress, Elementor, MySQL
  • Tools & Technologies: Git, GitHub, VS Code, and Figma
  • Other Knowledge Areas: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, API Integrations

I bring a full-stack approach to each project, ensuring seamless integration between front-end user experience and back-end functionality.

Alongside my work in development, I am an active content creator with over 85K followers on Instagram. Through my channel, Tamil Ponnu in London, I blend humor, cultural insights, and personal experiences, connecting with a global audience. Collaborating with brands like Lemfi, Vance, and RworldExpress, I’ve successfully created promotional content that’s not only entertaining but also results-driven.

My experience in content creation has taught me the power of storytelling, which I bring to my development work. Each project, whether it’s a codebase or a video, is an opportunity to share a narrative and engage users meaningfully.

Creativity, collaboration and a commitment to quality drive everything I do. I believe that great work comes from a strong balance of structure and innovation. Whether it’s brainstorming solutions with a team or diving into solo problem-solving, I prioritize clear communication, reliable processes, and, most importantly, a passion for constant learning. I’m always excited to explore new frameworks and methods to enhance my skill set and deliver better results.

When I’m not coding or creating content, you’ll likely find me exploring new cities, capturing moments on my camera, or attending tech meetups. A favorite memory of mine is capturing a photo in front of the Eiffel Tower on my first visit to Paris—a reminder of the beauty of blending work with life’s adventures!
